There are new openings in the project for which we are looking for participants.

Who are we looking for?

People who enjoy working with computers and software and want to learn an ICT profession. Our specialty is Software Testing. We write programs that automate website testing.

Our Profession

Testers check product quality before it reaches the end user. Does it work? Does it break? Is it user-friendly? Those kinds of things. Everything we find is reported back to the product designers so improvements can be made. Software testing is an integral part of the software development process. Below is a video of one of our automated tests.

Our team writes scripts to automate website testing. Every action a user can perform in a browser, we script. This is an example of such a test.

On the left side of the screen, you see the script running, and on the right side, you see how the script interacts with the client’s website. During execution, the script continuously checks whether the site responds correctly and whether the data in the browser is accurate.

What do we offer?

Our client is a multinational active in the automotive aftermarket. We test the garage management software developed for our client in India.

We work as a Scrum team together with a stakeholder and the India team. Our client provides professional testing facilities so we can offer a realistic learning and working environment. You will use top-tier tools and test the software on the RC and staging environments of the application.

What will you do?

  • The first 4 weeks are for orientation and study. You will learn the basics of web testing and test scripting.
  • You will join the daily Scrum meeting and the bi-weekly review with the stakeholder and the India team.
  • You will analyze daily test runs, document your findings, and improve existing code if needed.
  • You will contribute to writing new test scripts.
  • You will work on your own portfolio project.
  • You will explore the job market and identify where your career opportunities lie.
